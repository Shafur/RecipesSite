### Ingredients
- 3 cups self-rising flour  
- 3 tablespoons sugar  
- 1 can beer (12 oz., at room temperature)  

### Instructions
1. Mix well.  
2. Pour into a greased loaf pan.  
3. Bake at 350Â°F for about 40 minutes.  
4. Rub margarine on top when you remove from oven.
